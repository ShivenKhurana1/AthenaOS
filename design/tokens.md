# Design tokens

These tokens define the AthenaOS glasskit look and feel. They should be used across the shell, apps, and marketing assets.

## Usage

- Use glass tokens for surfaces and overlays
- Use ink tokens for readable text on glass
- Use motion tokens for short, gentle transitions

## Notes

- Keep translucency subtle to avoid eye strain
- Increase contrast when glass overlays text-heavy content
- Use the accent colors sparingly as focus cues
